Hotel Information

The reunion planning committee has selected the Embassy Suites by Hilton Cincinnati-RiverCenter as our host hotel for the family reunion. The Embassy Suites by Hilton Cincinnati-RiverCenter is just a two-minute drive from downtown Cincinnati, nearby attractions like Paul Brown Stadium, The Banks, Kings Island/Soak City Amusement Parks, Newport Aquarium, Fountain Square, and The Aronoff Center for the Arts. The hotel is also only 2.7 miles from the excitement of Jack Casino. The hotel offers free weekday shuttle services within a 2-mile radius from 6 to 10 AM and 4 to 10 PM. There is no shuttle service to and from the airport.

The Southbank Shuttle Trolley stops outside the hotel, offering easy access to downtown, restaurants, and shopping areas. Cincinnati/Northern Kentucky International Airport is only 20 minutes away and the hotel is has easy access to I-71, I-75 and many Cincinnati, OH and Covington, KY attractions.
